  • If all of your past neighbors have become used to the sound of screaming, shouting and smashing from your house and this has caused huge embarrassment to you and your children, D Now!
  • If every time when you walk out of your house, you feel a heavy burden being lifted from your chest and you feel like a freed bird flying out of its cage, D Now!

  • If in the heat of argument, you are pretty sure that if there were a loaded gun nearby, you would not hesitate to grab the gun and shoot your spouse. And your spouse may very likely do the same. If so, D Now!
  • If no old photos and loving memories of the past can rekindle your feeling of passion towards your spouse, D Now!
  • If this marriage has made you hate your life and lose interest in life, if it makes you think of death and suicide, D Now!
